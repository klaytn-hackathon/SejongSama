Winning Prize

When you guessed the scores right, you can claim your winning prize by clicking Claim Prize button from My History page. We will then send you the prize directly from the contract.

Here is the formula how your prize will be calculated from the smart contract. There is no trick, it is very simple.

((Betting Price * Number of Participants) / Number of Winners) - Commission (3%)

Use Case

If you bet on a 0.4 KLAY match and there were in total 13 participants. Let's say you guessed the scores right and there was one more person who also won the bet, then it will be:

Your prize: (0.4 * 13) / 2 = 2.6 KLAY
Commission: 3% commission from your prize, 2.6 * 0.03 = 0.078 KLAY

So as a final winning prize, you will be getting 2.6 - 0.078 = 2.522 KLAY
